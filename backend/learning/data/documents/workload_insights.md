# Workload and Learning Correlation (Synthetic)

> **Demo data only** — Work IQ signal patterns for engagement scheduling.

## Insights

- Employees with more than **20 meeting hours per week** show lower study completion rates.
- Optimal completion appears when learners have **12–18 meeting hours** and at least **15 focus hours**.
- Morning learners (preferred slot) complete 15% more weekly study blocks than evening learners under high meeting load.
